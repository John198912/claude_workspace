# Claude 3 Model Family Overview

Anthropic announced the Claude 3 family on March 4, 2024, introducing three models with increasing capability levels.

## Model Variants

**claude-haiku-4-5-20251001** represents the most capable tier, excelling at complex reasoning tasks. Pricing is $15 per million input tokens and $75 per output tokens, with a 200K context window (1M available for select customers).

**Claude 3 Sonnet** balances intelligence and speed for enterprise workloads at $3 input/$15 output per million tokens. It's positioned as 2x faster than Claude 2/2.1 while maintaining higher intelligence.

**Claude 3 Haiku** prioritizes speed and cost-effectiveness at $0.25 input/$1.25 output per million tokens, capable of processing dense research papers in under three seconds.

## Key Capabilities

The models demonstrate near-human comprehension on complex tasks and improved performance across analysis, forecasting, content creation, and code generation. They support multiple languages including Spanish, Japanese, and French.

Vision capabilities allow processing photos, charts, graphs, and technical diagrams—valuable for enterprise customers with knowledge bases in PDF and presentation formats.

## Performance Improvements

Compared to Claude 2.1, Opus shows twofold accuracy improvements on factual questions while reducing hallucinations. The models exhibit "near-perfect recall" exceeding 99% accuracy on the Needle In A Haystack evaluation.

Refusals decreased significantly; the models demonstrate more nuanced understanding of requests and refuse harmless prompts less frequently than previous generations.

## Availability

Opus and Sonnet launched immediately via the Claude API (now generally available in 159 countries) and claude.ai. Haiku availability was forthcoming. Integration with Amazon Bedrock and Google Cloud's Vertex AI was underway.
